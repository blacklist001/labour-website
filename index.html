Client Flow

From search to review

  1. Sign up or log in.
  2. Choose service and see workers near you.
  3. View ratings, experience, price, photos, and availability.
  4. Hire, pay online or in cash, then rate and review.

Worker Flow

Register, get verified, receive jobs

  1. Add personal details, phone, and ID verification.
  2. Choose profession and optional education or skills.
  3. Set location, working hours, and previous work photos.
  4. Receive job requests and get paid.
