Terms & Conditions

Last updated: June 26, 2026

These Terms & Conditions explain the rules for using LABOUR. By creating an account, booking a worker, registering as a worker, or using the platform, you agree to these terms.

Platform Role

LABOUR helps clients find workers and helps workers receive job requests. Unless separately agreed in writing, LABOUR is a marketplace platform and is not the direct employer of workers or the direct provider of listed services.

Accounts

Users must provide accurate account information and keep login details secure. LABOUR may restrict or remove accounts that provide false information, misuse the platform, or create safety risks.

Worker Profiles and Verification

Workers are responsible for providing accurate service, experience, location, price, availability, and photo information. Admin approval means the profile was reviewed for platform listing, but clients should still confirm job details before work begins.

Bookings

Clients are responsible for giving clear job details, correct contact information, accurate location information, and reasonable access to the work site. Workers are responsible for accepting only jobs they can complete safely and professionally.

Payments

Payment may be tracked as cash, M-Pesa, card, unpaid, pending, paid, failed, or refunded depending on the job and integration status. Users should confirm the final amount before work begins. M-Pesa payment confirmation depends on Safaricom Daraja responses and may take time to update.

Reviews

Clients should leave honest reviews based on completed work. Reviews that are abusive, false, discriminatory, or unrelated to the job may be removed.

Safety and Conduct

Users must not use LABOUR for illegal activity, harassment, fraud, unsafe work, impersonation, or discrimination. Clients and workers should communicate respectfully and take reasonable safety precautions when meeting in person.

Service Quality

Workers are responsible for their workmanship, tools, timing, and professional conduct. Clients are responsible for inspecting completed work and raising concerns promptly.

Changes and Availability

LABOUR may update services, features, prices, policies, or availability over time. The platform may be unavailable during maintenance, third-party outages, payment provider issues, or technical problems.

Important Note

These starter terms should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before a full public launch, especially before processing production payments or offering sensitive domestic service categories.
