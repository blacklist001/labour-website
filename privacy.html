Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how LABOUR collects, uses, stores, and protects information when clients, workers, and admins use the platform.

Information We Collect

We may collect account details such as name, email address, phone number, role, preferred language, and login information. For workers, we may collect service type, location name, experience, pricing, availability, working hours, photos, profile descriptions, verification status, and ratings.

For bookings, we may collect job descriptions, job location, contact phone, preferred time, selected worker, payment method, payment status, M-Pesa references, and review details.

How We Use Information

We use this information to create accounts, show verified workers, process bookings, support job communication, track payments, improve trust and safety, display ratings and reviews, and help admins review worker profiles.

Payments

When M-Pesa is used, LABOUR may store payment status, checkout request IDs, receipt references, phone numbers used for payment, and payment result messages. Sensitive payment credentials are handled through secure server-side functions and should not be stored in public frontend code.

Sharing Information

Relevant booking details may be shared between the client and assigned worker so the job can be completed. Public worker cards may show approved worker profile details, service type, location name, ratings, availability, and uploaded work photos.

Data Security

LABOUR uses Supabase authentication, database rules, and private server environment variables to protect user data. No online system is completely risk-free, so users should keep their login details private and report suspicious activity.

User Choices

Users can update profile details, reset passwords, and request changes to inaccurate information. Workers can update their worker profile and photos, subject to admin review where required.

Children and Domestic Services

Domestic service categories such as babysitting and caregiving require responsible use by clients and workers. LABOUR may require additional verification before making sensitive service categories broadly available.

Contact

Use the Help chat on the website for support. Add an official support email or phone number before public launch.
